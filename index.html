VidaPay Quick Guide
⬆️ Activation — You Pay
When you activate a customer, VidaPay charges you a fee — less than the plan price.
$150 plan → you pay $138
⬇️ Instant Spiff — You Get Paid
Right after activation VidaPay sends commission back. More comes over 6 months.
$88 paid back on $150 plan
📦 Subsidy — Phone Refund
If you gave the customer a phone, VidaPay refunds the phone cost after activation. Enter the IMEI and it auto-fills.
iPhone 13 IMEI entered → $259.99 auto-filled
🔄 Refill — You Keep 8%
Customer pays their monthly refill. VidaPay takes 92%, you keep 8%.
$25 collected → $2.00 yours · $40 → $3.20
🚀 400% MRC Bonus
Hit your monthly goal and every activation earns 4x more commission until month end.
Hit goal → commission multiplies 4x
🔗 IMEI Links Phone to Rebate
Add a phone to inventory with its IMEI and rebate amount. When you activate with that IMEI, the subsidy fills automatically.
Enter IMEI → rebate auto-populated instantly
